About Pause Former

"Pause - Former" is an platformer game where you use the pause and sliders to complete obstacles. Strategize your next move with pause button and sliders to complete the level.

  • Pause Button as a obstacles
  • Press option button to use sliders
  • Gravity slider to adjust the gravity
  • Brightness slider to adjust the brightness
  • Move the sliders as an obstacle to complete levels
